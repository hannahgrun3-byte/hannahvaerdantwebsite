A 20-minute self-audit for founder-led service businesses.

HANNAH verdant

Most growing businesses don’t have a marketing problem. They have a clarity problem.

Marketing usually grows up alongside a business rather than being designed on purpose. The pieces exist, they’re just not joined up.

This audit takes about 20 minutes. Seven areas, three questions each. Score every question 0 to 2, honestly. Nobody sees this but you.

How to score

0rarely / no
1sometimes / partially
2yes / consistently

Your score

Before you add anything up, circle your lowest-scoring section. That’s usually the one quietly dragging on all the others, and the one worth talking about first.

0–14
